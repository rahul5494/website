Education Overview

Classes 1-5

Our primary education focuses on foundational skills such as reading, writing, and mathematics, fostering a love for learning through interactive and engaging methods.

Classes 6-8

Middle school provides a more comprehensive syllabus that includes science, social studies, and languages, encouraging critical thinking and collaboration.

Classes 9-12

Our high school curriculum prepares students for higher education and careers, offering advanced subjects and specialized tracks in science, arts, and technology.

Syllabus Highlights

Mathematics

From basic arithmetic to calculus, we ensure a solid understanding of mathematical principles.

Science

Our science curriculum includes biology, chemistry, and physics, with hands-on experiments in our well-equipped labs.

Languages

We offer a robust language program that includes English and regional languages, focusing on both literature and practical usage.

Arts

Our arts program encourages creativity through music, painting, and drama, nurturing the artistic talents of our students.

Laboratories

Our school is equipped with state-of-the-art laboratories for physics, chemistry, biology, and computer science, allowing students to engage in practical learning and experiments.
